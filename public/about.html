Building Flood Resilience with Data & AI.

RisqMap is a US-focused initiative providing an accessible, timely flood early warning platform. We bring together previously siloed sources — river gauges, rainfall radar, satellite indicators, and community reports — into a single operational view for emergency managers and the public.

Background

Flood risk can escalate rapidly in urban watersheds. Telemetry is often available from multiple agencies but is fragmented across systems. RisqMap aims to bridge these gaps and deliver actionable lead time to communities and responders.

Our approach treats safety information as a public good. By combining modern telemetry, hydrologic models, and machine learning, we provide earlier and clearer guidance to help protect people and property.
