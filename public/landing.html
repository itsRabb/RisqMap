About RisqMap

RisqMap is a real-time flood early warning platform tailored for the United States. We combine IoT telemetry, public datasets (USGS, NWS, FEMA), satellite-derived indicators, and machine learning to provide actionable flood insights for municipalities, emergency managers, and communities.

RisqMap is developed by RisqMap Systems, an applied data engineering team focused on delivering operational tools for disaster preparedness and response. Our mission is to make flood risk information timely, transparent, and easy to act on.

Future integrations include automated alerting, shelter coordination, and an open API for partners and developers.
